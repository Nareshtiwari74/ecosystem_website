Threats & Challenges

There are several threats and challenges that faced to protect our ecosystem:-

Climate change: Global warming and climate change pose significant threats to ecosystems worldwide, altering the distribution and abundance of species, and affecting the availability of resources. This could result in ecosystem collapse and biodiversity loss.


Habitat destruction:

Human activities such as deforestation, mining, urbanization, and agriculture have led to the destruction and fragmentation of natural habitats. This results in the loss of biodiversity and affects the ecological functioning of ecosystems.
